Welcome to Contiv

Contiv offers the quickest, easiest way to manage and scale your container infrastructure. A ready-to-go networking platform designed to be centrally managed by enterprise IT, with developer self-service, Contiv provides a higher level of networking abstraction for microservices. Contiv secures your application using a rich policy framework. It provides built-in service discovery and service routing for scale out services.
